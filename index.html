Part Three: Great Big Loveums

Bored with the endless silence of Francis, Kippums pressed onwards. Beyond the clearing was a path, leading to a valley and a lake.

Sweet lovely Kippums walked along the lakeside, enjoying the look of the waterlilies and sassy frogs. She called out to nature: "Cooo coo!" Nature did not answer. However, she heard a rustling from deep within a grassy nub.

"Hello! It's me, Mr. Rock!" exclaimed Mr. Rock. "I have something extremely important to exclaim!"

Kippums was astounded. That was nothing but gibberish! I wonder what on earth Mr. Rock could be on about?

Mr. Rock continued to exclaim: "They call me Julius, or JC for short. You aren't going to stab me in the back, are you?"
